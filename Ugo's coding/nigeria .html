Nigeria

Ndewo (this means welcome in Igob which is one of the many languages is nigeria)My name is Ugochi, I was born and raised in Ireland, but my heritage is Nigerian. My parents, courageous immigrants, instilled in me a deep pride in our roots, a pride that blossomed into a profound love for my ancestral land. Nigeria is also divided into tribes i am apart of the igbo tribe. Being Nigerian, born and raised in Ireland, is not a contradiction; it's a beautiful blend of two worlds, two cultures, two homes. It's a testament to the power of heritage and the beauty of embracing one's identity fully. I am proudly Nigerian, proudly Irish – and eternally grateful for the rich tapestry of my life.
